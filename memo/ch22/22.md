# 22章で扱う体系

## 項

$$
\begin{aligned}
   t \enspace ::= \enspace &x \\
   & \lambda x:T.t \\
   & t \space t \\
   & true \\
   & false \\
   & if \space t \space then \space t \space else \space t \\
   & 0 \\
   & succ \space t \\
   & pred \space t \\
   & iszero \space t
\end{aligned}
$$

## 値

$$
\begin{aligned}
   v \enspace ::= \enspace &\lambda x:T.t \\
   & true \\
   & false \\
   & nv
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
   nv \enspace ::= \enspace &0 \\
   & succ \space nv
\end{aligned}
$$

## 型

$$
\begin{aligned}
   T \enspace ::= \enspace &T \rightarrow T \\
   & Bool \\
   & Nat \\
   & A
\end{aligned}
$$
